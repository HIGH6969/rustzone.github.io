Server Rules

Follow these rules to ensure a fair and enjoyable experience for everyone

No Intentional Bug Abuse

Abusing glitches/bugs to gain an unfair advantage is not allowed.

Streamsniping

Streamsniping of content creators partnered with Rustoria, whether you are aware they are partnered or not, is not permitted.

No racism, homophobia, spamming in text chat, or real world trading

We're wanting to offer a comfortable place to play Rust here at Rustoria. With this, we have filters and moderation in place to prevent the use of Racism and Homophobia. Also, if you're continuously spamming the chat we'll hand out chat based restrictions. Any breach of our chat-based rules will result in chat-based restrictions being handed out, with multiple convictions resulting in longer Rustoria wide bans. The selling of in-game services or resources for real world currency is not permitted. This includes the advertisement of such services in chat.

Do not wall off Outpost!

Due to us merging Outpost & Bandit together on our servers, Outpost is an important monument on the map. Therefore, we do not allow walling off of the Outpost on our servers. If you're found to do this, all walls involved in walling off the outpost will be removed without ANY compensation.

No Rust EAC/Gamebanned Alts

You are NOT allowed on our Vanilla servers if your situation is one or more of the following: You have a gameban on another account within the last 180 days, You have more than 1 gameban on other accounts, You have an account that is banned on our servers and are playing on another account. We are able to check this, and if we find out, you will be banned. Generally, the only reason you will have a banned account is because you were cheating. False-positives are extremely rare, so please don't argue about this.

Electricity Restrictions [Long Only]

Due to the recent electricity issues we no longer allow you to pass water through 2 or more grids to your main/farm base. We will be removing such water setups whenever issues occur.

No Excessive Toxicity

Conduct that is beyond an unacceptable level is not tolerated on our servers. This includes, but is not limited to: Doxing, DDoS threats, death threats, sexual harassment or excessive toxicity that goes beyond acceptable levels, subject to admin discretion.

No cheating and/or scripting

Cheating is defined as using any paid or free programs that provide assistance by interacting with the game. This includes aim-assist, wall-hacks, speed-hacks and any other advantage provided by such an application. This extends to scripting, as scripting provides you with an advantage that you would not normally have. This includes scripts that are used to combat recoil, but does not include having keybinds to automatically hit trees, as this can be set up in-game without external assistance. Playing with cheaters, ban evaders or benefitting from the presence of cheaters by association, deliberately or not, is not permitted.
